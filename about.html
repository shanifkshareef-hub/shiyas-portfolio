About Shiyas Kareem.

An Alluring Blend of Elegance and Athleticism.

    Shiyas Kareem, the accomplished Indian model and actor, embarked on his remarkable journey in the fashion industry in 2010, transitioning from a promising football career. Since then, he has graced prestigious runways across India and internationally, earning accolades such as Best Model in the All India Model Hunt.

    Shiyas Kareem
    Glamour to Glory
    Notably representing India at the Mr. Grand Sea international Model Hunt 2018, Shiyas secured titles like 'Mr. Popularity' and 'Best Photo Model.' His foray into acting includes notable roles in films like 'Veeram' and 'Captain,' where he portrayed a football player. A sought-after figure in the fashion realm, Shiyas has collaborated with renowned brands like Peter England and Raymond.
